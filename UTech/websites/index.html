introduction

Urban Technology has been established in Skardu near Olding Masjid and Imam Bargah Khargrong with a revolutionary idea, to promote IT sector locally in Skardu and baltistan region. We are focused to fill the gap of the formal education of School/colleges/universities and the market requirements of IT industry through skill development by trained instructors who have in-depth knowledge of the practical market and IT industry and thanks for AKRSP to help with us.
